U.S. Presidential Election Results by County (2004-2020)
Github

This is a simple, responsive map that displays the democratic vote share by county for the five U.S. Presidential Elections between 2004 and 2020.

Select a year below to see the democratic vote share for each county in the United States for that election year.

You can also click on the map to see which county it is.

Select Year

Data for this map was taken from the MIT Election Data Lab.

Legend

0% - 20%
20% - 40%
40% - 60%
60% - 80%
80% - 100%
